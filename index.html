Skills and Jobs

We're searching for an IT Project Manager that is structured and detail-oriented to join our team. The IT Project Manager is responsible for monitoring the software project from beginning to end, managing and encouraging software developers and other team members, making client presentations, and creating project proposals.

To be successful as an IT Project Manager, you should have an excellent working knowledge of project estimation techniques and the ability to motivate team members. Ultimately you will have good communication and decision-making skills.

Job Responsibilities

  • Discussing projects and the requirements with clients and software developers

  • Planning out the roadmap for software projects including defining the scope, allocating resources, setting deadlines, defining communication strategies, and indicating tests and maintenance requirements

  • Bringing together and guiding the project team

  • Taking part in and overseeing each stage of the project

  • Ensuring the project stays on schedule and adheres to the deadlines

  • Developing a project budget and ensuring that the project stays as close to the budget as possible

  • Identifying and overseeing consistent testing, review, and troubleshooting of all products at all stages of development

  • Keeping track of deadlines, deliverables, and requests for changes

  • Assisting in the communication of changes, milestones reached, and other important details

Job Requirements

  • A bachelor's degree in IT, software development management, software engineering, computer science, or a similar discipline

  • Proven project management and software development experience

  • Good understanding of project estimation methods

  • Excellent technical understanding

  • Leadership, decision-making, and organisational skills are all important

  • Strong multitasking and attention to detail skills

We’re looking for a seasoned front-end developer who has a philanthropist mindset and the ability to take over a project and make it their own. You will have a proven track record of bringing projects to market as well as finding a solution to a difficult problem.

Ideally, you’ll have experience working autonomously. Your technical expertise will see you manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables. You will design, develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance software solutions.
